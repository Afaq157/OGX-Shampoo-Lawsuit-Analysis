## Overview

The [ogx shampoo lawsuit](https://lawsuitzone.com/ogx-lawsuit/) refers to legal and consumer complaints against OGX, a popular hair care brand, regarding product safety, effectiveness, and alleged misleading labeling. This repository compiles available information, public discussions, and legal documentation related to the case.

---

## Background

OGX shampoo is widely used for hair care and styling. Over the years, some consumers have reported negative experiences, including:

- Hair damage or breakage  
- Adverse reactions  
- Claims that marketing misrepresented product benefits  

These complaints led to public attention and, in some cases, legal action.
